# 📊 Supply Chain Performance Analytics Dashboard

An interactive business intelligence dashboard built to monitor and optimize supply chain operations through data-driven insights.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is a dynamic **Power BI dashboard** designed to analyze end-to-end supply chain performance. It provides actionable insights into suppliers, inventory, shipments, and customers to support strategic decision-making and operational efficiency.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Monitor supply chain KPIs in real time
* Identify high-cost suppliers and delayed shipments
* Track inventory levels and product defects
* Evaluate logistics and delivery performance
* Analyze customer revenue contribution

---

## 🧩 Dashboard Modules

### 🏠 Executive Overview

Displays key performance indicators and overall supply chain trends.

### 🏭 Supplier Performance Analytics

Analyzes supplier cost, quality score, lead time, and order efficiency.

### 📦 Inventory Performance Analytics

Tracks stock levels, safety thresholds, defect rates, and product movement.

### 🚚 Shipment & Logistics Analytics

Evaluates carrier performance, shipment cost, delivery efficiency, and delays.

### 👥 Customer Revenue Analytics

Shows revenue distribution, profit trends, and top customer contribution.

---

## 📈 Key Insights Generated

* Identified cost-efficient suppliers with high quality scores
* Detected carriers responsible for maximum delivery delays
* Discovered slow-moving inventory impacting storage cost
* Highlighted top revenue-contributing customers
* Analyzed sales channel performance for optimization
